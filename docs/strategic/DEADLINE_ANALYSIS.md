## Federal Fiscal Year vs Calendar Year

**Federal Fiscal Year:** October 1 - September 30
- FY 2025: October 1, 2024 - September 30, 2025
- FY 2026: October 1, 2025 - September 30, 2026

**Calendar Year:** January 1 - December 31
- 2025: January 1, 2025 - December 31, 2025
- 2026: January 1, 2026 - December 31, 2026

**Key Point:** Federal agencies operate on fiscal years, not calendar years. December 31, 2025 is just another day in FY 2026.
