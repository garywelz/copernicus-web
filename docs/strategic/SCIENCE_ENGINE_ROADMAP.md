# Science Knowledge Engine: Complete Roadmap

**Goal:** Build unified Science Knowledge Engine covering Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science, and Biology before moving to Medicine.

---

## Strategy Overview

**Approach:** Complete all science disciplines first, then unify into a single Science Knowledge Engine. This creates:
- Cohesive science knowledge base
- Strong cross-disciplinary connections
- Foundation for Medicine (which bridges science and healthcare)
- Natural progression: Science → Medicine → Applied Domains
