# Knowledge Engine Expansion Strategy

**Goal:** Identify and prioritize additional Knowledge Engines beyond Mathematics

---

## Expansion Philosophy

**Core Principle:** Clone the Mathematics Knowledge Engine architecture to other domains, adapting:
- Content sources (domain-specific databases)
- Concept extraction (domain-specific ontologies)
- Process visualization (domain-specific processes)
- Evaluation metrics (domain-specific benchmarks)

**Success Factors:**
1. **Rich Content Sources** - Large, accessible databases
2. **Clear Concepts** - Well-defined domain concepts
3. **Process Knowledge** - Structured processes to visualize
4. **Community Need** - Active research community
5. **Low Hanging Fruit** - Easier to implement first

---
