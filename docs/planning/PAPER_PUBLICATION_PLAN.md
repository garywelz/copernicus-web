## Writing Guidelines

### Style
- **Clear and Concise:** Avoid unnecessary jargon
- **Evidence-Based:** Ground claims in operational prototypes
- **Accessible:** Write for broader audience when possible
- **Honest:** Acknowledge limitations and challenges
- **Collaborative:** Encourage participation and adoption

### Structure
- Clear introduction with problem statement
- Methodology section with sufficient detail
- Results with concrete examples
- Discussion of implications and limitations
- Conclusion with future directions

### Figures and Tables
- Process diagrams (Mermaid examples)
- System architecture diagrams
- Statistics and metrics tables
- Screenshots of interfaces (if applicable)
- Citation network visualizations

### Citations
- Cite relevant prior work
- Reference other CopernicusAI components (cross-citation)
- Include links to operational prototypes
- Cite foundational papers in relevant fields
