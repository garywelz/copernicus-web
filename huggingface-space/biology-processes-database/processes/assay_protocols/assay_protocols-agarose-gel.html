Agarose Gel Electrophoresis

Biology Assay / Lab Protocols Complexity: high
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Agarose gel electrophoresis: DNA fragments loaded alongside a size ladder migrate through an agarose matrix toward the anode and separate by size, are stained and visualised under UV, and re-run at adjusted conditions if bands are not resolved.
