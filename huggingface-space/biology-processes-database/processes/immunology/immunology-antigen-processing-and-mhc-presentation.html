Antigen Processing and MHC Presentation

Biology Immunology Complexity: high
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Antigen presentation: endogenous proteins are processed by the proteasome and loaded onto MHC class I for CD8+ T cells, while exogenous antigens are degraded in endosomes and loaded onto MHC class II for CD4+ T cells.
