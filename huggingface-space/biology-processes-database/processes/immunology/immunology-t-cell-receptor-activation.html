T Cell Receptor Activation

Biology Immunology Complexity: high
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

T-cell activation: TCR engagement of peptide-MHC plus CD28 costimulation triggers Lck/ZAP-70 signaling and the LAT signalosome, branching into Ca2+/NFAT, NF-kB, and MAPK pathways that drive IL-2 and clonal expansion; without costimulation the cell becomes anergic.
