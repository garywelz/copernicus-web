Innate Immune Pattern Recognition

Biology Immunology Complexity: high
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Pattern recognition: PRRs detect microbial PAMPs, and surface vs endosomal receptors recruit MyD88 or TRIF adaptors, activating NF-kB and IRF transcription factors to induce cytokines and type I interferons.
