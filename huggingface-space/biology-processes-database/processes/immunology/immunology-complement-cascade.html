Complement Cascade

Biology Immunology Complexity: high
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Complement activation: classical, lectin, and alternative pathways converge on C3 convertase, driving opsonization, anaphylatoxin release, and assembly of the C5b-C9 membrane attack complex that lyses pathogens.
