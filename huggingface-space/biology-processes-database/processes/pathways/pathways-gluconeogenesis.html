Gluconeogenesis

Biology Pathways Complexity: medium
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Gluconeogenesis: pyruvate is converted to glucose by bypassing the three irreversible glycolytic steps (pyruvate carboxylase/PEPCK, fructose-1,6-bisphosphatase, glucose-6-phosphatase), gated by energy charge and reciprocal hormonal regulation in liver.
