Cell Cycle (G1–S–G2–M)

Biology Pathways Complexity: high
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Cell cycle: cyclin-CDK complexes drive progression through G1, S, G2 and M, with the G1/S restriction point, the G2/M DNA-integrity checkpoint and the spindle-assembly checkpoint arresting the cycle for repair when conditions are not met.
