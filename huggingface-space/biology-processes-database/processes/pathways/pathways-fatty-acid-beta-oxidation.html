Fatty Acid Beta-Oxidation

Biology Pathways Complexity: medium
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Fatty acid beta-oxidation: activated fatty acyl-CoA is shuttled into the mitochondrion by carnitine, then each cycle of oxidation, hydration, oxidation and thiolytic cleavage shortens the chain by two carbons, yielding acetyl-CoA, FADH2 and NADH.
