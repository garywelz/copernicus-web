Photosynthesis Light Reactions

Biology Pathways Complexity: high

Description

Light reactions: photons excite Photosystem II, water is split to replace electrons and release O2, electrons travel the Z-scheme through cytochrome b6f and Photosystem I to make NADPH, and the proton gradient drives ATP synthesis.
