Pentose Phosphate Pathway

Biology Pathways Complexity: medium
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Pentose phosphate pathway: glucose-6-phosphate enters the oxidative branch (G6PD, rate-limiting) to generate NADPH and ribose precursors, and the non-oxidative branch interconverts sugars to balance NADPH versus ribose-5-phosphate demand.
