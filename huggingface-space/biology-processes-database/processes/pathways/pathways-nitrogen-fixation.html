Nitrogen Fixation

Biology Pathways Complexity: medium
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Biological nitrogen fixation: the nitrogenase Fe protein uses ATP to deliver electrons one at a time to the MoFe protein's FeMo-cofactor, which accumulates enough electrons and protons to reduce inert N2 to two ammonia, later assimilated by GS/GOGAT.
