Wnt / Beta-Catenin Signaling

Biology Pathways Complexity: high
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Wnt/beta-catenin signaling: without Wnt, the Axin/APC/GSK3 destruction complex phosphorylates beta-catenin for degradation; Wnt binding to Frizzled/LRP recruits Dishevelled, inhibits the complex, and lets beta-catenin accumulate and activate TCF/LEF target genes.
