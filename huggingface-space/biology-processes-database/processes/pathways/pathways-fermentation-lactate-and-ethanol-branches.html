Fermentation: Lactate and Ethanol Branches

Biology Pathways Complexity: medium
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Fermentation: when oxygen is limiting, cells regenerate NAD+ to sustain glycolysis by reducing pyruvate, either to lactate via lactate dehydrogenase (animals) or to ethanol via pyruvate decarboxylase and alcohol dehydrogenase (yeast).
