CRISPR–Cas9 Mechanism

Biology Mechanisms Complexity: high
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

CRISPR-Cas9: a guide-RNA-programmed Cas9 nuclease locates a PAM, checks guide-target complementarity, and cleaves both DNA strands, creating a double-strand break repaired by NHEJ or HDR to edit the locus.
