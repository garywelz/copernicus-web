Trp Operon (Attenuation)

Biology Mechanisms Complexity: high
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Trp operon control: high tryptophan lets the Trp repressor block the operator, while at low tryptophan transcription proceeds and attenuation via leader-peptide ribosome stalling decides whether RNA polymerase reads through to the structural genes.
