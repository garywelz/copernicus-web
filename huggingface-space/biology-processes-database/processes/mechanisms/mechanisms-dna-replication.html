DNA Replication (Leading and Lagging Strands)

Biology Mechanisms Complexity: medium
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

DNA replication: helicase unwinds the origin, primase/polymerase synthesize the continuous leading strand and discontinuous Okazaki fragments on the lagging strand, which are matured and ligated as the fork advances.
