Plant Hormone Auxin Signaling

Biology Development and Organismal Biology Complexity: medium
← Back to Biology Database Programming Framework

Description

Auxin signaling: rising auxin promotes TIR1/AFB-mediated ubiquitination and degradation of Aux/IAA repressors, freeing ARF transcription factors to drive auxin-response genes, with negative feedback from re-synthesized repressors.
