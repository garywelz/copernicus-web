Quantum Harmonic Oscillator

Physics Quantum Mechanics Complexity: high
← Back to Physics Database Programming Framework

Description

The quantum harmonic oscillator is solved algebraically with ladder operators: rewriting the Hamiltonian in terms of a and a-dagger whose commutator is one gives H = hbar*omega(a-dagger a + 1/2); the ground state is annihilated by a, and repeated raising builds the equally spaced spectrum E_n = hbar*omega(n + 1/2).
