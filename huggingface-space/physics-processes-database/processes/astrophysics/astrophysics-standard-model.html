Stellar Nucleosynthesis (pp-chain)

Physics Astrophysics Complexity: high
← Back to Physics Database Programming Framework

Description

A protostar becomes a main-sequence star through hydrogen fusion: gravitational contraction heats the core until the proton-proton chain ignites, fusing hydrogen to helium-4 and releasing energy by mass defect; the resulting radiation pressure balances gravity, establishing hydrostatic equilibrium.
