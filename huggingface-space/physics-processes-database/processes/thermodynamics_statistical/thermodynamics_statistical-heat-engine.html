Carnot Heat Engine

Physics Thermodynamics & Statistical Physics Complexity: high
← Back to Physics Database Programming Framework

Description

The Carnot engine sets the maximum efficiency of any heat engine: a working substance runs a reversible cycle of isothermal expansion absorbing heat from the hot reservoir, adiabatic expansion, isothermal compression rejecting heat to the cold reservoir, and adiabatic compression, giving efficiency eta = 1 - Tc/Th.
