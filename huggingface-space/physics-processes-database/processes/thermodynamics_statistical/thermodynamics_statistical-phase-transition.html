Ising Model Phase Transition

Description

The Ising model captures a continuous phase transition: from the spin Hamiltonian the partition function gives the free energy and the magnetization order parameter; below the critical temperature the system spontaneously breaks symmetry into an ordered phase, with critical exponents describing behavior near Tc.
