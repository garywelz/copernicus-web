Phonons & Debye Heat Capacity

Physics Solid State Physics Complexity: high
← Back to Physics Database Programming Framework

Description

Lattice vibrations quantized as phonons set a crystal's heat capacity: normal modes have a dispersion that the Debye model approximates as linear up to a cutoff frequency; integrating the phonon density of states gives a specific heat scaling as T-cubed at low temperature and approaching the Dulong-Petit value at high temperature.
