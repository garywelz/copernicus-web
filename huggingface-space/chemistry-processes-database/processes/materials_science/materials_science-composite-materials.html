Composite Stiffness (Rule of Mixtures)

Chemistry Materials Science Complexity: medium
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

The rule of mixtures predicts the elastic stiffness of a continuous-fiber composite from constituent properties and volume fractions: loading parallel to the fibers obeys the iso-strain Voigt bound while transverse loading obeys the iso-stress Reuss bound, with corrections for fiber orientation giving the effective modulus.
