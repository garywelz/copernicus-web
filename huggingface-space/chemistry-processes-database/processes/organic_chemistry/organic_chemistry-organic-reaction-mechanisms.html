Nucleophilic Substitution: SN1 vs SN2

Chemistry Organic Chemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

Whether an aliphatic nucleophilic substitution follows SN1 or SN2 depends mainly on substrate class: methyl/primary substrates favor concerted backside SN2 with inversion, tertiary favor stepwise SN1 via a carbocation with racemization, and secondary depend on conditions.
