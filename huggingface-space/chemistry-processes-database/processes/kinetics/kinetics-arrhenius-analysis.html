Arrhenius Plot for Activation Energy

Chemistry Kinetic Processes Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

Extract the activation energy and pre-exponential factor by measuring rate constants at several temperatures, linearizing the Arrhenius equation as ln k versus 1/T, and reading the activation energy from the slope and the pre-factor from the intercept.
