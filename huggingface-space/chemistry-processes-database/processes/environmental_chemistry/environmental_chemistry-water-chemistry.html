Carbonate System & Alkalinity

Chemistry Environmental Chemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

Natural water chemistry is governed by the carbonate system: dissolved CO2 forms carbonic acid that dissociates stepwise to bicarbonate and carbonate, setting speciation versus pH; treating the system as open (fixed pCO2) or closed (fixed total carbon) and applying mass and charge balance yields the equilibrium pH and alkalinity.
