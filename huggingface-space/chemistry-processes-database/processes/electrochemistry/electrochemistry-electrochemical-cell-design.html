Galvanic Cell & the Nernst Equation

Chemistry Electrochemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

Design a galvanic cell by assigning anode and cathode half-reactions, computing the standard cell potential, correcting for non-standard conditions with the Nernst equation, and judging spontaneity from the sign of the cell potential.
