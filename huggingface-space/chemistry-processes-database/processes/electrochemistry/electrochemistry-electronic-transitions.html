Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy

Chemistry Electrochemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

EIS applies a small AC perturbation across a range of frequencies, measures the complex impedance, and fits Nyquist/Bode data to an equivalent circuit to extract charge-transfer resistance, double-layer capacitance and diffusional (Warburg) behavior.
