Organometallic Catalytic Cycle

Chemistry Inorganic Chemistry Complexity: high

Description

Homogeneous organometallic catalysis turns over through elementary organometallic steps - substrate coordination, oxidative addition, migratory insertion, and reductive elimination - with the 16/18-electron rule governing whether a vacant site is available for the next step.
