Sol-Gel Synthesis

Chemistry Materials Chemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

Sol-gel processing converts metal alkoxide precursors into an oxide network through hydrolysis and condensation: a colloidal sol crosslinks into a continuous gel, which is aged and then dried either supercritically to an aerogel or by evaporation to a xerogel, and finally calcined to the dense oxide.
