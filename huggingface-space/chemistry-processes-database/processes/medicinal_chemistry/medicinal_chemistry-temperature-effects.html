Hit-to-Lead ADMET Optimization

Chemistry Medicinal Chemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

Hit-to-lead progression: confirm and triage hits, assess potency, profile ADMET, and iteratively modify structure to satisfy drug-likeness, improve solubility and metabolic stability, and clear toxicity flags before nominating a lead series.
