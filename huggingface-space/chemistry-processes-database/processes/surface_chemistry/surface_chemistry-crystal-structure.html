Surface Reconstruction & Relaxation

Chemistry Surface Chemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

A freshly created surface has high-energy dangling bonds; to lower its energy the top layers relax (change spacing) or reconstruct (rebond into a new periodicity), behavior that adsorbates can further alter and that is probed by LEED or STM.
