Heterogeneous Catalysis (Langmuir–Hinshelwood)

Chemistry Surface Chemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

In the Langmuir-Hinshelwood mechanism both reactants adsorb on the catalyst surface, diffuse and react in the adsorbed state, and the product desorbs to regenerate the active site, giving a catalytic turnover cycle.
