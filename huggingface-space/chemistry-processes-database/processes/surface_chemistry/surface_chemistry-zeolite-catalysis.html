Zeolite Shape-Selective Catalysis

Chemistry Surface Chemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

Zeolites impose shape selectivity through their micropores: reactants must fit the aperture to reach Brønsted acid sites, the transition state must fit the cavity, and only products that can exit are released, giving reactant, transition-state and product selectivity.
