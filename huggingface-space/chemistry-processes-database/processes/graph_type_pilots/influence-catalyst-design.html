Description

Computational catalyst design links structure to performance through reactivity descriptors: the active-site structure sets the d-band center, which shifts adsorption energy; linear scaling relations tie adsorption energy to activation barriers and turnover frequency, and the Sabatier principle predicts an optimal intermediate binding that, balanced against selectivity and stability, determines overall catalyst performance.
