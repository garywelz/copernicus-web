Harmonic Vibrational (IR) Frequencies

Chemistry Quantum Chemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

Vibrational frequencies follow from the Hessian at an optimized geometry: mass-weighting and diagonalization give normal modes; the absence of imaginary frequencies confirms a minimum, IR intensities come from dipole derivatives, and scaled frequencies simulate the IR spectrum.
