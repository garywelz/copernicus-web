Molecular Orbitals via LCAO

Chemistry Quantum Chemistry Complexity: high
← Back to Chemistry Database Programming Framework

Description

Molecular orbitals are built from atomic orbitals: only AOs of matching symmetry and similar energy combine, generating bonding and antibonding MOs that are ordered by energy and filled by the Aufbau principle to give bond order and an MO diagram.
