# Biology Processes Database - Suggested Process List

**Purpose:** Create a comprehensive database of higher-level biological processes for complex organisms, complementing GLMP's focus on biochemical/molecular processes. This database will cover organismal, developmental, behavioral, ecological, and physiological processes.

**Target:** 50+ processes across diverse organisms (plants, animals, fungi, protists)

---

## Suggested Process Categories & Processes

### 1. Reproduction & Development (10-12 processes)

#### Sexual Reproduction
1. **Mating Behavior Process** (Animals) - Courtship, mate selection, copulation behaviors
2. **Fertilization Process** (Animals) - Sperm-egg interaction, membrane fusion, zygote formation
3. **Pollination Process** (Plants) - Pollen transfer, stigma reception, germination
4. **Self-Incompatibility Response** (Plants) - Recognition and rejection of self-pollen
5. **Sexual Selection Process** (Animals) - Mate choice mechanisms, sexual dimorphism evolution

#### Development
6. **Embryonic Development Process** (Animals) - Cleavage, gastrulation, organogenesis stages
7. **Metamorphosis Process** (Amphibians/Insects) - Larval to adult transformation
8. **Seed Germination Process** (Plants) - Dormancy break, radicle emergence, seedling establishment
9. **Flowering Process** (Plants) - Floral induction, organ development, anthesis

---

### 2. Growth & Morphogenesis (8-10 processes)

10. **Tissue Regeneration Process** (Animals) - Wound healing, cell replacement, organ regrowth
11. **Apical Meristem Development** (Plants) - Shoot/root tip growth, cell division patterning
12. **Molting Process** (Arthropods) - Exoskeleton shedding, new cuticle formation
13. **Skeletal Growth Process** (Vertebrates) - Bone formation, remodeling, ossification
14. **Branching Morphogenesis** (Plants/Animals) - Lung, kidney, root branching patterns
15. **Leaf Development Process** (Plants) - Primordia initiation, lamina expansion, senescence
16. **Limbs Formation Process** (Animals) - Limb bud development, digit formation

---

### 3. Behavior & Communication (8-10 processes)

17. **Migration Process** (Animals) - Navigation, orientation, seasonal movement patterns
18. **Hibernation Process** (Animals) - Metabolic suppression, temperature regulation, arousal
19. **Foraging Behavior Process** (Animals) - Food search, capture, handling strategies
20. **Social Hierarchy Establishment** (Animals) - Dominance behaviors, rank determination
21. **Communication Signal Production** (Animals) - Vocalization, pheromone release, visual displays
22. **Nest Building Process** (Animals) - Material selection, construction, maintenance
23. **Parental Care Process** (Animals) - Offspring protection, feeding, teaching behaviors
24. **Symbiotic Relationship Formation** (Multiple) - Mutualism establishment, recognition, maintenance

### 4. Defense & Immune Response (6-8 processes)

25. **Innate Immune Response Process** (Animals) - Pattern recognition, inflammation, phagocytosis
26. **Adaptive Immune Response Process** (Animals) - Antigen presentation, lymphocyte activation, memory formation
27. **Plant Defense Response Process** (Plants) - Hypersensitive response, systemic acquired resistance
28. **Camouflage Process** (Animals) - Coloration matching, background adaptation
29. **Mimicry Process** (Animals) - Batesian/Müllerian mimicry, predator avoidance
30. **Chemical Defense Production** (Plants/Animals) - Toxin synthesis, sequestration, release

### 5. Nutrition & Metabolism (8-10 processes)

31. **Digestion Process** (Animals) - Food breakdown, nutrient absorption, waste elimination
32. **Photosynthesis Process** (Plants/Algae) - Light capture, carbon fixation, O₂ production
33. **Nitrogen Fixation Process** (Plants/Bacteria) - Atmospheric N₂ conversion, nodule formation
34. **Root Nutrient Uptake Process** (Plants) - Ion transport, mycorrhizal interaction
35. **Predation Process** (Animals) - Prey capture, handling, consumption
36. **Filter Feeding Process** (Animals) - Suspension feeding, particle capture mechanisms
37. **Symbiotic Nutrition Process** (Multiple) - Mycorrhizal, gut microbiome interactions

### 6. Sensory & Perception (6-8 processes)

38. **Vision Process** (Animals) - Photoreception, image formation, visual processing
39. **Hearing Process** (Animals) - Sound wave reception, frequency analysis, localization
40. **Olfaction Process** (Animals) - Odorant detection, receptor binding, signal transduction
41. **Gustation Process** (Animals) - Taste receptor activation, flavor perception
42. **Tactile Sensation Process** (Animals) - Touch receptor activation, pressure detection
43. **Photoperiod Sensing Process** (Plants/Animals) - Day length detection, circadian rhythm entrainment

---

### 7. Transport & Circulation (6-8 processes)

44. **Blood Circulation Process** (Animals) - Heart pumping, vascular flow, gas exchange
45. **Lymphatic System Process** (Animals) - Fluid transport, immune cell circulation
46. **Xylem Transport Process** (Plants) - Water and mineral upward movement
47. **Phloem Transport Process** (Plants) - Sugar translocation, source-sink dynamics
48. **Gas Exchange Process** (Animals/Plants) - Respiration, transpiration, diffusion
49. **Osmoregulation Process** (Animals) - Water balance, ion regulation, excretion

---

### 8. Coordination & Control (6-8 processes)

50. **Endocrine Signaling Process** (Animals) - Hormone release, target tissue response
51. **Nervous System Signaling Process** (Animals) - Action potential, synaptic transmission
52. **Plant Hormone Response Process** (Plants) - Auxin, cytokinin, gibberellin actions
53. **Tropism Response Process** (Plants) - Phototropism, gravitropism, thigmotropism
54. **Circadian Rhythm Process** (Multiple) - Internal clock regulation, periodicity maintenance
55. **Homeostatic Regulation Process** (Animals) - Temperature, pH, glucose balance

---

### 9. Adaptation & Evolution (5-6 processes)

56. **Acclimatization Process** (Multiple) - Environmental stress adaptation, phenotypic plasticity
57. **Dormancy Process** (Plants/Animals) - Metabolic arrest, environmental cue response
58. **Diapause Process** (Insects) - Developmental arrest, overwintering preparation
59. **Antifreeze Production Process** (Animals) - Cold tolerance mechanisms
60. **Desiccation Tolerance Process** (Plants/Animals) - Water loss resistance, recovery

---

### 10. Ecological Interactions (5-6 processes)

61. **Competition Process** (Multiple) - Resource competition, competitive exclusion
62. **Predator-Prey Interaction Process** (Animals) - Chase, escape, capture dynamics
63. **Parasitism Process** (Multiple) - Host location, infection, resource exploitation
64. **Succession Process** (Communities) - Community change, species replacement
65. **Decomposition Process** (Multiple) - Organic matter breakdown, nutrient cycling

## Notes

- GLMP focuses on molecular/biochemical processes (DNA replication, protein synthesis, metabolism pathways)
- This database focuses on organismal/ecological processes (behaviors, development, interactions)
- Processes can span multiple organisms when conceptually similar
- Each process will include organism specificity in metadata
- 5-color scheme will be applied consistently (same as other discipline databases)
