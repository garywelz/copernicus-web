Description

Dynamic programming for sequence alignment. Needleman-Wunsch (1970): global alignment of entire sequences; recurrence F(i,j) = max{F(i-1,j-1)+s, F(i-1,j)-g, F(i,j-1)-g}; traceback from (m,n). Smith-Waterman (1981): local alignment; set negative scores to 0; traceback from max cell until 0. Both O(mn) time and space. Scoring: match bonus, mismatch/gap penalties. Foundation for BLAST and alignment tools.
