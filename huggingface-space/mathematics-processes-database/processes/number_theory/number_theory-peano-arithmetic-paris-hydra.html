Description

Hercules-Hydra game: Hydra is a rooted finite tree; Hercules chops a leaf; Hydra grows n copies of the subtree above the chopped node's parent (n = distance from root). Hercules always wins, but termination is unprovable in PA. Proof: assign ordinals to trees; each move strictly decreases ordinal; requires ε₀ induction.

Source: Kirby, L. & Paris, J., "Accessible Independence Results for Peano Arithmetic", Bull. London Math. Soc. 14 (1982)
