Description

Homomorphisms with layered dependencies. Definitions: homomorphism φ(ab)=φ(a)φ(b), kernel, image, normal subgroup. Kernel and image are subgroups; kernel is normal. Kernel trivial iff φ injective. First isomorphism theorem: G/ker φ ≅ im φ. Cayley's theorem: every group embeds in Sym(G). Second isomorphism theorem (H,K ≤ G, K normal: HK/K ≅ H/(H∩K)). Third isomorphism theorem (K ≤ H ≤ G normal: G/H ≅ (G/K)/(H/K)).

Source: Isomorphism theorems (Wikipedia); Dummit & Foote
