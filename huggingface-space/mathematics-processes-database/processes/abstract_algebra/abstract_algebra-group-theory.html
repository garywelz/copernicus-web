Group Theory

Axiomatic development of group theory, structured like Euclid's Elements. Four group axioms at the base; theorems build in layers with explicit dependencies. Chart 1: axioms through power laws (17 nodes). Chart 2: subgroup definitions, subgroup criteria, cosets partition, Lagrange, corollaries (14 nodes). Chart 3: homomorphisms, kernel, image, normal subgroup, FIT, Cayley, 2nd & 3rd isomorphism theorems (12 nodes).
