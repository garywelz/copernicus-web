Description

Three Hilbert axioms, modus ponens, self-implication, double negation, transposition, hypothetical syllogism. Shows how theorems depend on axioms, definitions, and prior theorems.

Source: Frege, G. Begriffsschrift (1879); Łukasiewicz, J. Elements of Mathematical Logic (1929)
