Description

The Measurable Riemann Mapping Theorem (MRMT): For a Beltrami differential μ on ℂ with ‖μ‖∞ < 1, the Beltrami equation ∂̄f = μ ∂f has a unique quasiconformal solution f: ℂ→ℂ fixing 0, 1, ∞. Equivalently: any measurable conformal structure on ℂ̂ is realized by a unique quasiconformal map. Central to Sullivan's no wandering domain proof: deform conformal structure along orbits to produce contradictions.

Source: Wikipedia; Ahlfors–Bers
