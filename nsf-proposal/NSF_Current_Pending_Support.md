## Overlap and Conflict of Interest

**No Overlap or Conflict:** There are no current or pending sources of support that would create overlap or conflict of interest with the proposed NSF research.

**Self-Funded Prior Work:**
The existing CopernicusAI Knowledge Engine components represent prior work that has been entirely self-funded. These prototypes serve as the foundation and proof-of-concept for the proposed research, but the proposed NSF research will extend significantly beyond the current prototypes through:

1. **Research Briefing Interface Design:** New research on iterative refinement loops and evaluation frameworks (beyond current operational system)
2. **Unified Metadata Representation:** Development of canonical schemas and cross-modal linking algorithms (beyond current separate databases)
3. **Reliability Mechanisms:** Research on multi-checker workflows and error detection systems (new research component)
4. **Process Visualization Integration:** Extension of Programming Framework and integration research (beyond current GLMP application)
5. **Evaluation Studies:** User studies and algorithmic evaluation (new research component)

The proposed NSF research represents a significant expansion into research questions, evaluation methodologies, and systematic study that goes well beyond the current operational prototypes.
