## Budget Justification Summary

This budget supports a 3-year research project to develop and evaluate an AI-enabled assisted research platform. The budget allocates:

- **35% to Senior Personnel:** PI effort (6 months/year)
- **15% to Other Personnel:** Research assistant and graduate student user testers
- **10% to Fringe Benefits:** CUNY GC rates (PI summer 25.05%; part-time 8% + 0.895% MTA)
- **22% to Other Direct Costs:** Cloud infrastructure, consultants, materials ($15,000), publication, other
- **34% to Indirect Costs:** CUNY GC research rate (54% of MTDC)

**Totals:** Total Direct Costs $453,029; Total Indirect Costs $238,155; **Total Request $691,184.**
