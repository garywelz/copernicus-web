# CopernicusAI — One-Pager (VC / Investor)

**Tagline:** AI-powered research briefings and a structured knowledge layer so researchers get oriented faster and trace every claim to its source.

---

## Problem

Research output is exploding (papers, preprints, videos, podcasts), but tools are siloed. Researchers and advanced students waste time on orientation, re-finding the same literature, and redoing informal synthesis. Generic AI chat doesn't ground answers in sources or expose provenance—so it's not trusted for serious work.

## Solution

**CopernicusAI** is a subscription-grade assisted-research platform that:

- **Generates AI research briefings** (5–10 min audio + transcript) grounded in real sources, with provenance and iterative refinement ("ask narrower, ask deeper").
- **Builds a structured metadata layer** (papers, videos, briefings, process diagrams) so users can navigate across modalities and see knowledge graphs, not just search results.
- **Exposes APIs and MCP** so institutions and third-party tools can plug in (institutional licenses, developer ecosystem).

We're not another chatbot: we're a **knowledge engine**—orientation, synthesis, and traceability in one place.

## Vision: Knowledge Engines in Any Domain

The Knowledge Engine design is **not limited to science**. The same architecture—grounded briefings, structured metadata, provenance, knowledge graph—applies to any domain. We prove it with CopernicusAI (science); the playbook extends to:

- **HippocratesAI** — Medicine: PubMed and other medical sources; insight and decision support for clinicians and researchers.
- **CiceroAI** — Law: Lexis Nexis and firms' private files; orientation and synthesis for legal teams.
- **SocratesAI** — Philosophy: historical and contemporary literature; support for scholars and students.
- **ThucydidesAI** — History: historical and contemporary sources; research and narrative synthesis.
- **YogiAI** — Baseball (Yogi Berra): historical and recent data; tool for managers, fans, and analysts.

Knowledge Engines can exist in **any domain** and provide **insight and decision support**. CopernicusAI is the first instantiation; the platform multiplies TAM across verticals (medicine $1–3B+, law $30B+ / legal research $2–9B+, humanities ~$100M–400M, sports analytics $2–4B+). See MARKET_RESEARCH_TAM_SAM_SOM.md.
