## Slide 3: Solution (What We Build)

- **Research briefings:** 5–10 min AI-generated audio briefings grounded in real sources, with provenance and “ask narrower, ask deeper”
- **Structured metadata layer:** Papers, videos, briefings, process diagrams in one graph—navigate across modalities, not just search
- **APIs & MCP:** Institutional licenses and developer ecosystem (third-party tools, AI assistants)
- One line: *We’re a knowledge engine: orientation + synthesis + traceability.*
