## Budget Justification Summary

This budget supports a 6-month Phase I effort to enhance the existing CopernicusAI platform with:
- Enhanced evidence-grounded research briefing pipeline
- Structured metadata object generation
- Cross-modal linking capabilities
- Quality and reliability subsystems

The budget allocates 48% to personnel (reflecting the technical nature of the work), 18% to cloud infrastructure (necessary for production-scale development), and includes appropriate allocations for consultants, travel, and indirect costs.

**Key Cost Drivers:**
1. **Personnel (48%):** Full-time PI effort plus development support
2. **Cloud Infrastructure (18%):** Production GCP services and LLM API costs
3. **Subcontracts (12%):** Specialized expertise in metadata design and QA

This budget is consistent with typical DOE SBIR Phase I awards and supports the technical objectives outlined in the proposal.
