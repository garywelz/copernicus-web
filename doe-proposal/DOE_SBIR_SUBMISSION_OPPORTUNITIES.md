## Which Topics to Apply For

The CopernicusAI proposal (AI-powered research briefings, structured metadata, knowledge graphs, RAG, multi-modal scientific discovery) fits best under:

1. **Advanced Scientific Computing Research (ASCR)**  
   Scientific AI, data and software for science, tools for scientific discovery, knowledge systems. Check for subtopics on data analytics, AI/ML for science, or scientific software.

2. **Biological & Environmental Research (BER)**  
   If topics include tools for literature/data synthesis, metadata, or research workflows in life or environmental sciences.

3. **Other Office of Science program areas**  
   When the topic list is published, scan for any mention of: scientific discovery tools, research synthesis, metadata/knowledge graphs, AI for research, or data integration.

**When the FOA is available:** Download the topic PDF, pick the single best-matching topic (and subtopic if any), and tailor the narrative’s opening and keywords to that topic. Page limits and section order are in the FOA.
